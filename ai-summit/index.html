About the Summit

A Weekend Built for Clarity, Connection, and Action

The AI Summit at Riverside Farm is an intentionally curated three-day experience for professionals, entrepreneurs, and innovators who want to move beyond the noise and develop a clear, practical understanding of Artificial Intelligence.

Set against the backdrop of one of Vermont's most stunning private estates, this summit blends world-class learning with the calm, focused energy that only a true retreat environment can provide. No distractions. No filler. Just meaningful conversations and actionable insight.

Attendees will hear from AI leaders, practitioners, and educators sharing real-world strategies across business, technology, and personal productivity — and just as importantly, they'll share their own experiences with each other.

The Core Outcome

Leave with Your Own AI Playbook

One of the defining outcomes of this summit is the development of a practical, personalized AI playbook. This isn't a generic handout — it's a working document you build throughout the weekend, shaped by your specific goals, industry, and challenges.

By Sunday afternoon, every participant walks away with a clear roadmap for implementing AI in meaningful ways — immediately.

  • ✓ Proven AI frameworks adapted to your context
  • ✓ Prioritized implementation roadmap
  • ✓ Curated tool stack for your use cases
  • ✓ Efficiency and automation opportunities identified
  • ✓ 90-day action plan with accountability structure
