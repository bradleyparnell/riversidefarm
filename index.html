The Estate

A Private World,
Reserved for You

Riverside Farm is not a conference center. It is a 700-acre private Vermont estate — three magnificently restored 1800s barns, sweeping mountain views, and a dedicated event team that handles every last detail.

While the farm is celebrated for its wedding legacy, its landscapes, barns, and professional infrastructure make it equally extraordinary for corporate gatherings. When your company books Riverside Farm, you have it entirely to yourselves — no shared lobbies, no competing agendas, no distractions.

Three Ways to Lead

Whether your team needs sharp focus, bold adventure, or deep restoration — the estate bends to fit your vision.

01

Executive Summits & Boardroom Strategy

Gather leadership teams in the Red Barn's grand hall — timber beams above, meadows beyond the French doors. Full A/V, dedicated event staff, and farm-to-table catering keep every session sharp and every meal inspired.

02

Adventure & Team Building

700 acres of Vermont wilderness become your playground. Hiking, trail challenges, bonfire evenings, yoga on the meadow, and custom adventure programming designed to forge real trust between real people.

03

Wellness & Leadership Retreats

Mountain air, unhurried mornings, and space to think differently. Curated wellness programs, group coaching sessions, and evening bonfires under Vermont stars help leaders reconnect with what drives them.
